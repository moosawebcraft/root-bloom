Our story

We cook backwards — from the soil up.

Before Root & Bloom had a menu, it had a map of farms. We spent a year visiting organic growers across Punjab before we ever opened the doors, and those relationships still decide what's cooked tonight — not a printed menu.

Our kitchen is led by chefs trained across Copenhagen and Singapore, who returned home to build a menu around Punjab's own growing seasons — a handful of ingredients, treated with patience, in a room built to slow you down.
