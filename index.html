Our philosophy

We cook backwards — from the soil up.

Before Root & Bloom had a menu, it had a map of farms. We spent a year visiting growers across Punjab before we ever opened the doors, and those relationships still decide what's cooked tonight — not a printed menu.

It means our kitchen plans around harvests instead of forcing them. Some dishes last a single week. Others return every summer. Nothing is flown in from somewhere else just to be available year-round.

Our chef

Amara Rehman

Head Chef & Culinary Director

"Cook with what's outside the door, and the plate takes care of itself."

Amara spent seven years in kitchens across Copenhagen and Singapore before returning to Lahore to build a menu around Punjab's own growing seasons. Her food is deliberately quiet — a handful of ingredients, treated with patience.
